The only annual Winter Ball at The University Of Cambridge

Every year, Selwyn College is transformed into a magical venue, bursting with musical performances, riveting activities, and of course, limitless supplies of food and drinks. The Snowball has become the most highly anticipated event of Cambridge’s Michaelmas term, and we can’t wait to share what we have planned for the 2018 event.

Also, note that even if you were lucky enough to get tickets for the Varsity Trip you can still attend the ball, as there are special late coaches for Snowball guests.

Check out our Facebook for more details
