Snowball Ticketing Information

Tickets for Selwyn Snowball 2018 will go on sale on Saturday 27th October. Selwyn students can buy tickets from 10am with general release to all Cambridge students at 12am. (Selwyn Students must use their Cambridge email to access the system). The link to purchase tickets will be shared on our Facebook page and on our website. Please note that payment will be required immediately in order to secure tickets. Tickets include unlimited drinks and food, alongside spectacular entertainment and incredible music. Ticket prices are:
- General: £85 for current Selwyn students (and their guests); £89 for others (+ booking fee)
- VIP: £95 for current Selwyn students (and their guests); £99 for others (+ booking fee)
VIP tickets include a champagne reception in Selwyn College Chapel and early entry to the Ball. You are limited to purchase a maximum of either:
- 2x VIP tickets
- 4x General tickets
If you are a Selwyn student, in order to purchase your guest allocation you must also purchase a ticket for yourself. We reserve the right to deny access to the ball in the event that we believe a Selwyn student is unfairly using guest allocations. If this limitation presents an issue, please contact: ticketing@selwynsnowball.co.uk

Payment Information

Payment will only be accepted using Agora's payment system (i.e. paying by credit or debit card).
Unlike previous years, payment will be required immediately.
If paying by card presents an issue, please make sure that you let us know in advance by contacting ticketing@selwynsnowball.co.uk
